Our Story

Every seam,
drawn by hand.

Corset Atelier began with a single pattern block and a conviction that structure could still feel like softness. Every piece is cut, boned and laced in-house — nothing is mass produced, nothing leaves the atelier without a hand having checked every eyelet.
