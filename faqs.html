FAQs

Sizing & Fit

Check our Size Guide for the full chart and measuring instructions. If you're between sizes or want a guaranteed fit, use the Custom Builder's custom measurements option instead.
Yes — the Custom Builder has a dedicated step for bust, waist, hip, and torso length, and every piece is then cut to those numbers specifically.

Materials & Care

Depending on the piece: silk satin, duchess satin, cotton coutil, velvet, French lace, and silk brocade, all fully boned with steel boning and lined for structure and comfort.
Spot clean only — avoid machine washing or soaking, as this can damage the boning and shape. Store flat or loosely laced on a hanger, away from direct sunlight.

Custom Orders & Pricing

Design your piece in the Custom Builder, confirm details with us on WhatsApp, then pay a 70% deposit to begin. Production takes 17 days, with the remaining 30% due before delivery. Full details are on our Custom Order Policy page.
Ready-made pieces generally range from Rs. 6,000 to Rs. 22,000+ depending on category and fabric — luxury and bridal pieces sit at the higher end. Custom builds are quoted based on your fabric and design choices.
Before production starts, yes — message us as soon as possible. Once production has begun, the deposit is non-refundable since the piece is cut specifically for you.

Shipping & Delivery

Ready-made pieces typically ship within 3–5 days. Custom pieces take 17 days for production before they ship. See our Shipping & Returns page for full details.
Cash on Delivery (COD) across Pakistan. Orders are placed and confirmed through WhatsApp.

Styling & Fashion Advice

Waist trainers work well under fitted dresses, tucked shirts, or for shaping under eveningwear. For all-day comfort, our everyday cotton coutil styles are the most breathable option.
Our Bridal collection is designed to sit smooth under a gown — ivory or blush silk satin pairs beautifully with most bridal fabrics. Message us with your gown style and we're happy to advise.
