How to Measure Yourself

For the most accurate fit, measure over light clothing with a soft measuring tape, standing naturally — don't pull the tape tight.

Bust

Measure around the fullest part of your bust, keeping the tape level around your back.

Waist

Measure around your natural waistline — the narrowest point, usually just above the belly button.

Hip

Measure around the fullest part of your hips, roughly 8" below your natural waist.

Between Sizes, or Want a True Custom Fit?

If your measurements fall between two sizes, or you'd like a corset built to your exact numbers rather than a standard size, use the Custom Builder — it has a dedicated custom measurements step for bust, waist, hip, and torso length.
