The Atelier

Our Story

Structure, softened. A brand built one hand-boned seam at a time.

Corset Atelier began with a single pattern block and a conviction that structure could still feel like softness. What started as one maker cutting and boning corsets by hand has grown into a small atelier — but the process hasn't changed. Every piece that leaves us is still cut, boned, and laced in-house, checked eyelet by eyelet before it ever reaches a customer.

We work in small batches, made to order rather than mass-produced. That means every corset is built for the person who ordered it — their size, their color, their fabric — not pulled off a rack. It takes longer than fast fashion. We think that's the point.

What We Believe

Corsetry has always sat at the intersection of engineering and softness — steel and silk, structure and comfort. We design toward that balance rather than away from it.

Handcrafted, Not Mass-Produced

Every corset is cut and boned by hand in the atelier — nothing comes off an assembly line.

Built to Last

Steel boning, real coutil, and hand-finished eyelets — construction that holds its shape order after order.

Made For You

Standard sizing where it fits, and a true custom-measurement build when it doesn't.
